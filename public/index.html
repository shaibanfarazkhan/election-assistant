Election Timeline

Typical phases of an Indian General Election

1

Announcement

Election Commission announces dates. Model Code of Conduct begins.

2

Nominations

Candidates file their nomination papers.

3

Campaigning

Parties and candidates campaign. Ends 48h before polling.

4

Polling Day

Citizens cast their votes using EVMs across multiple phases.

5

Results

Counting of votes and declaration of the final results.
